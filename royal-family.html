Jason

He wants revenge on his father and recover the throne of Lolcos. He is smart but cowardly and lazy, so he invents a story to stay at home and sends you to risk your life in his adventure.

Medea

The beautiful and young daughter of King Aeetes promised to Jason with the Golden Fleece.
